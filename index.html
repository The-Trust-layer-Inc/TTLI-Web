TTLI's initiative

Understand where current tools fail

Most fraud systems rely on static rules or outdated behavioral patterns. While they catch known threats, they often miss subtle, emerging ones or worse, block legitimate users. We're analyzing these blind spots to uncover what truly limits today's detection models.

Identify new detection opportunities

Fraud evolves faster than most security stacks. By studying real-world cases and team workflows, we aim to surface new signals, data relationships, and behavioral markers that could power the next generation of adaptive fraud detection.

Build a shared framework for adaptive fraud intelligence

The goal isn't just another tool — it's a foundation. A shared, flexible framework that helps platforms learn, adapt, and respond to fraud dynamically, without slowing down user experience or growth.
